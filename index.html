Hotel

Personal hotel recommendation

Hotel Lancelot

Hotel lancelot has many famous landmarks local to it such as the colosseum etc. It is within an easy walking walking distance from many places such as religious places, cafe bars and more. This makes the neighbourhood feel authentic making it easier to get along with the community

  • location: Threads
  • Price a night: €150.00
  • Rating: 4.6/5 from 400 - Google



Hotel Artemide

offers modern and comfortable rooms, a rooftop terrace with a hot tub, a fitness center, and a renowned on-site restaurant serving Italian cuisine. It is located near the termini station. This makes traveling around Rome much easier as the station is just next door. Most noticeable feature is its paronamic view of Rome from the rooftop

  • location: Via Nazionale, 22, 00184 Roma RM
  • Price a night: €230.00
  • Rating: 4.8/5 from 1300 - Google



Hotel Quirinale

Provides spacious rooms with located near the Opera House. The hotel contains indoor fitness centre, on-site restaurant and many more. It represents a classic and elegance

  • location: Threads
  • price a night: Illustration
  • Rating: Illustration



Hotel Santa Maria

Renovated place of the 16th century that hosts beautiful courtyard garden. It is located in the Trastvere district which has many historic medieval houses and narrow alleyways. The neighbourhood is really active at night with the street buzzing full of tourists and restaurants.

  • location: Vicolo del Piede, 2, 00153 Roma RM
  • Price a night: €130.00
  • Rating: 4.9/5 from 600 - Google



Hotel Delle Nazioni

located near the Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps. It features elegant rooms, a stylish bar, a restaurant serving Italian and international cuisine. Due to it being located in the centre of Rome, it allows tourists to be given freedom to explore since most famous landmarks are local.

  • location: Via Poli, 6, 00187 Roma RM
  • Price a night: Illustration
  • Rating: Illustration

activites

Stuff to do while in Rome

Biking

Rome has many beautiful landscapes and buildings to immerse yourself in. Biking is a comfortable way to travel around Rome as it is easier to avoid traffic and sightsee. Some places to bike are:

  • Aniane Valley Park
  • The Appian Way
  • Castro Pretorio

    • price: €25/day





    Football

    Football is statistically the most famous sport in the world and Rome is no different. Football is valued in Italy a lot. fortunately football is not expensive either. All it takes is a circular soft ball to play football. There are famous football clubs in Rome such as "Roma" who are nicknamed "I Giallorossi" meaning "The Yellow and Red" because of their home colours which are Carmine Red and Golden Yellow

    • Ticket price: €45
